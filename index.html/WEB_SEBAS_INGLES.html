Party Time

Get ready for an unforgettable night! Join our incredible PartyTime tour and explore the best of Barcelona’s nightlife in the most epic way possible.

We’ll take you to 3 bars and 1 club from Sunday to Thursday, and on weekends, we turn up the intensity with 2 bars and 2 clubs on Fridays and Saturdays.

Every night includes welcome shots at each venue we visit (except the final club), all to kick off the party with energy! Plus, you’ll enjoy VIP access to all clubs, which means you can skip the line and walk straight into the city’s hottest parties.

Tour Time

Explore Barcelona with Our Free Walking Tours

Experience the magic of Barcelona with our free walking tours, led by expert local guides who will take you through the city’s most iconic and captivating sites.

From the awe-inspiring Sagrada Familia to the unique masterpieces of Gaudí, our tours will immerse you in the heart and soul of this vibrant city.

Stroll through the Gothic Quarter, where every street and square tells a story that spans centuries. Marvel at the panoramic views from Montjuïc, explore its lush gardens and historic fortresses, and end your day at the iconic Bunkers del Carmel, where you can enjoy a breathtaking 360° view of the city at sunset.

Our tours are free and tip-based, allowing you to decide the value of your experience by contributing what you feel our efforts are worth. Join us for an unforgettable adventure in Barcelona!
